In addition to desktops, laptops, tablets and smartphones, there are other types of computers used every day at home and work.

Wearables are devices that we 'wear' that are connected wirelessly to the internet. They use data to give us results based on why we are using them. Fitbit, SmartCap, Glass Enterprise are all examples of wearable technology.
